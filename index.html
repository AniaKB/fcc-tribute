Broken Patterned Rabbits

The Cutest Kind of Rabbits

Rabbits with the "broken" pattern

Rabbits not only come in different colors, but also different patterns. The "broken" pattern is arguably the most adorable pattern out there. White is usually the main color, with black or brown spots and ears. The broken pattern happens when the dominant gene for the broken pattern En mixes with the recessive solid color gene en. This produces rabbits with the En-en genotype. An en-en rabbit will produce a solid color kit, and an En-En will produce what is called a "Charlie", which is similar to a broken pattern but with less spots and mostly just coloring around the eyes and on the ears. If the ratio of black/brown is larger than the white color, it is known as a blanket pattern as opposed to spotted.
